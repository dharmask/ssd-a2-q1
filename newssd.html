About

Software System Development is a graduate-level course that equips students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, tools, and practices involved in designing and building modern software systems. Students will gain hands-on experience with Bash scripting, SQL, NoSQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Flask and more. The focus is on scalable, maintainable, and secure systems.
